ABOUT US

AARUNYA is India's firstever website dedicated to improving the health of all women and newborn babies. Aarunya provides clinical expertise and leadership in obstetrics, neonatal, gynaecology, oncology and mental health. We foster a holistic philosophy of health, provide national leadership in health promotion and consumer health communication and our website is an important advocate for improvements in women's health and well-being by providing patients with best ever treatment to support maternal health.
